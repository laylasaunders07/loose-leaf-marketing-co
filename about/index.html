about the team

Loose Leaf Marketing began as a one-person company until the founder (me) decided they wanted a change of scenery and redesigned the entire business, essentially starting right from scratch. Hiring her best friend to work on web design and to take care of the more technical sides of things, we are now a two-person, female-led business focusing on offering services such as marketing, advertising, graphic design, proofreading, editorial as well as merchandise design and distribution. We pride ourselves on being an AI-free business; our services are all 100% organic and human-made. In a world taken over by technology, we believe that real marketing and design comes from honest, hard work.
